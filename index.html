Biography

Hi! I am currently a Postdoctoral Researcher at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, advised by Prof. Anh Tuan Luu. I received my PhD from Jinan University (2021–2024) under the supervision of Prof. Jinming Wen. I spent one year at Jinan University and was jointly trained at Nanyang Technological University for the remaining period, under the supervision of Prof. Anh Tuan Luu.

🤔My research interests include Model Security, Agent, Backdoor Attack, Large Language Models. I keep collobration with my friends Dr. Yanhao Jia on Multi-Modal Tasks, Dr. Xinyi Wu on AI4Education, and Dr. Xuan Feng on Trustworthy AI.

🤝Note: I am always interested in working with self-motivated students. If you have a strong interest in large language model and model security, please feel free to contact me.

Systems

Projects

📌 Project 1 — Policy QA System (RAG-based)

Developed a domain-specific question answering system for public policy consultation, enabling users to retrieve accurate and up-to-date policy information through natural language queries.

  1. Designed document ingestion pipeline for structured and unstructured policy data
  2. Implemented retrieval optimization (chunking, embedding tuning, reranking)
  3. Improved answer factuality and reduced hallucination via grounded generation

🎓 Project 2 — University AI Assistant (NTU-GPT)

Designed and implemented a university-level AI assistant to support students and staff with academic, administrative, and campus-related queries.

  1. LLM-based responses for general queries
  2. RAG-based retrieval for factual / institutional knowledge

🧑‍🏫 Project 3 — Persona-based Conversational Agent

Created a persona-driven chatbot that simulates the speaking style and perspectives of a historical figure for educational and interactive purposes.

  1. Designed prompt engineering strategy to maintain persona consistency
  2. Curated and structured historical texts for grounding responses
  3. Combined LLM generation with retrieval to ensure factual alignment
